About Us

Angel Swimwear is an ethically made, sustainable swim company based out of Southern California. We create timeless, functional pieces that will withstand the changing fashion trends.

Angel Swimwear was founded in Spring of 2020, as a pet project our owner took on during quarantine. She saw a need to cultivate an affordable, high-end swimwear company that is focused on sustainability, women empowerment, and creativity. Our designs are functional, designed to stay on in and out of the water. They´re small production, with only 5 top and bottom designs, and 5 colorways, you know you will be a part of something new. Most colors and prints won´t be restocked, so get your hands on them before it´s too late!
